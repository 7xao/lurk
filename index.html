Key Features

Advanced Moderation

Comprehensive suite of moderation tools including kicks, bans, timeouts, warnings, and mass moderation capabilities. Automated protections against spam, links, invites, and raids to maintain a safe environment. Customizable filters and ignore lists for fine-grained control.

Economy System

Fully-featured economy with daily/weekly rewards, work, begging, crimes, robberies, and gambling games. Includes shops for boosts, black market trading, dealership for cars, housing market, stock trading, jewelry store, and limited items. Events and boosts to enhance earnings.

Fun & Games

Wide variety of entertainment commands including rock-paper-scissors, 8ball, polls, snipes, calculations, roasts, rizz lines, jokes, pickup lines, fortunes, compliments, and interactive games like trivia, music lyrics trivia, guess the country/flag/celebrity, tic-tac-toe, and more.

Utility Tools

Practical utilities like AFK status, custom prefixes, anti-features configuration, welcome/leave messages, auto-roles/responder/react, embed creation, logging, sticky messages, birthdays, self-purge, lookups, tickets, leveling, translations, booster roles, reminders, verification, weather, and encoding tools.

Bans and immediately unbans a member, deleting their recent messages.
